Group Information & Guidelines

Name

Joyep Entrepreneurial Group

A registered community-based organization focused on economic empowerment through collective savings and enterprise development.

Motto

"Where Prosperity is Personal"

Reflecting our belief that true wealth begins with individual discipline, amplified by community support.

Objectives

  • Promote savings culture among members
  • Empower members economically through access to capital
  • Promote unity, trust, and mutual accountability
  • Encourage financial discipline and literacy
  • Foster sustainable business development

Membership

Eligibility:

  • 18 years and above
  • Good character and integrity
  • Accepted by majority vote
  • Share common bond (locality, occupation, interest)

Admission: Registration fee: Ksh 500

Member Obligations

  • Attend monthly meetings consistently
  • Make regular savings contributions
  • Repay loans as per agreed policy
  • Participate in group activities & decisions
  • Uphold the group's values and reputation

Membership Termination

  • Missing 3 consecutive meetings without notice
  • Defaulting on loan repayments
  • Misconduct or breach of group constitution
  • Voluntary withdrawal with 30-day notice

All terminations require review and approval by the executive committee.

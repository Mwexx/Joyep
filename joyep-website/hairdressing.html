1. Hair Dressing & Beauty (Cosmetology)

Comprehensive program covering all aspects of professional hairdressing, beauty therapy, skincare, makeup artistry, and salon management. Graduates receive industry-recognized certification.

2. Beauty Therapy

Specialized training in facial treatments, body therapies, massage techniques, electrolysis, and wellness practices. Focus on holistic client care and advanced aesthetic procedures.

3. Hair Dressing

Master cutting, coloring, styling, braiding, weaving, and chemical treatments. Learn modern techniques, client consultation, and business skills for salon success.

4. Trainer of Trainers (TOT)

Advanced program for experienced professionals who wish to become certified instructors. Covers curriculum development, teaching methodologies, assessment, and mentorship skills.
